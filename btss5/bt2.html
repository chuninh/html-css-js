son tung



Nguyen Thanh Tung was born on July 5, 1994 in Thai Binh, commonly known by his stage name Son Tung M-TP. He is a Vietnamese singer, songwriter, rapper and actor. In his childhood, Tung often went to sing and learned to play the organ. After that, Tung together with his classmates formed a group, named Over Band, began to compose and post his songs on a website specializing in music called LadyKillah. Tung became famous when he released two singles "Con Mua Ngang Qua" and "Em cua ngay hom qua", marking the beginning of his career. Tung starred in the 2014 theatrical film Chang Trai Nam Ay and received a Canh Dieu Vang award in the Young Promising Actor category for his role. Son Tung possesses a very beautiful and inspiring voice. Although he is not a vocal expert, his voice is considered to be very catchy with young people and is suitable for his mainstream music, which has also brought in the largest number of young fans. Along with the advantage of appearance combined with vocal, he has a great attraction in the music market and music performances nationwide.
